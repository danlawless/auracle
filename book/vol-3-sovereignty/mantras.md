# Mantras for the Moment

## Grounding Phrases for Standing

*These are words you can return to when the ground feels uncertain beneath your feet.*

---

### When You Wait for Permission

- I don't need consensus to proceed.
- My authority is internal. It doesn't require witnesses.
- I can decide without being certain.
- No one else needs to approve of my choices for them to be valid.
- I am allowed to trust my own knowing.

---

### When Guilt Follows Choosing Yourself

- Choosing myself is not selfishness—it's sovereignty.
- I can disappoint others and still be kind.
- Their discomfort with my boundaries is not my emergency.
- I don't owe an explanation for protecting my peace.
- What is mine to carry ends where someone else's begins.

---

### When Calm Feels Wrong

- Stillness is not emptiness.
- The absence of urgency is not the absence of meaning.
- Peace can feel unfamiliar at first—that doesn't make it wrong.
- I don't need crisis to feel alive.
- Regulation is what I was always looking for.

---

### When You Absorb What Isn't Yours

- This emotion may be real—and may not be mine.
- I can witness without absorbing.
- Their storm doesn't have to become my storm.
- I am responsible for my experience. They are responsible for theirs.
- I can hold space without losing myself in it.

---

### When Someone Challenges Your Boundaries

- My no does not require their agreement.
- I don't have to justify what I'm not willing to do.
- Pushback is not proof I was wrong to set a limit.
- I can remain soft and still be firm.
- Boundaries are not walls. They're clarity.

---

### When You Want to Shrink

- I am allowed to take up space.
- I don't have to make myself smaller to be acceptable.
- My presence is not a burden.
- I can be visible without performing.
- What belongs to me deserves room.

---

### When Certainty Doesn't Come

- I can act without knowing the outcome.
- Clarity comes from movement, not from thinking harder.
- I trust the next step, not the whole staircase.
- Uncertainty is not the same as danger.
- I can tolerate not knowing.

---

### When Others Haven't Done the Work

- I can be sovereign in rooms that aren't.
- I don't have to match someone's energy to stay connected.
- Their dysregulation is not my assignment.
- I can care about someone without taking responsibility for their growth.
- I lead by regulation, not by control.

---

### The Foundation Mantras

*Return to these when nothing else reaches you:*

- Sovereignty is not separation. It's self-trust without urgency.
- What belongs to you can stay with you. What belongs to me can stay with me.
- I did not arrive here by becoming louder. I arrived here by learning how to stay.
- I am not small. I am learning to stand without fear.
- My authority lives inside me now.

---

*These mantras are reminders that you already know how to stand. You're just remembering.*
