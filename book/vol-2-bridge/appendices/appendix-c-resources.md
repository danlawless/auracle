## Crisis Resources

**If you are in immediate danger, please contact emergency services in your area.**

**Crisis Text Line**
- Text HOME to 741741
- Available 24/7

**SAMHSA National Helpline**
- Phone: 1-800-662-4357
- Substance abuse and mental health services referral
- Available 24/7

## Finding a Therapist

### What to Look For

When seeking a therapist for attachment and trauma work, look for:
- Training in attachment theory
- Trauma-informed approach
- Somatic or body-based modalities (helpful but not required)
- Experience with family systems
- Experience with narcissistic abuse recovery

### Therapy Modalities That May Help

**EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing)**
- Particularly effective for trauma processing
- Find a practitioner: emdria.org

**Somatic Experiencing**
- Body-based trauma resolution
- Find a practitioner: traumahealing.org

**Internal Family Systems (IFS)**
- Works with different "parts" of the self
- Find a practitioner: ifs-institute.com

**Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)**
- Attachment-based couples therapy
- Find a practitioner: iceeft.com

### Finding a Therapist

**Psychology Today Therapist Finder**
- psychologytoday.com/us/therapists
- Filter by specialty, insurance, and location

**Open Path Collective**
- openpathcollective.org
- Reduced-fee therapy for those with financial need
