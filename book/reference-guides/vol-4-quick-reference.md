## Daily Aura Hygiene

**Morning clearing:**
1. Cold water on face/wrists (resets vagal tone)
2. Deep exhale (activates parasympathetic)
3. Ground contact (bare feet if possible)
4. Intention: "I release what isn't mine"

**Evening clearing:**
1. Shake body for 30-60 seconds
2. Cold/warm shower alternation
3. Journaling: "What did I pick up today that isn't mine?"
4. Sleep with clear field

**After heavy interactions:**
- Physical movement (walk, shake, stretch)
- Cold water on inner wrists
- Breath: inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 8
- Mental release: "That's theirs to carry"

## The Sovereign Yes and No

**The Sovereign Yes:**
- Full body agreement
- Expansion in the chest
- Clarity without urgency
- "I choose this"

**The Sovereign No:**
- Full body refusal
- Contraction or closing
- Clarity without guilt
- "This isn't for me"

**The Maybe:**
- Requires more information
- Neither expansion nor contraction
- "I need time"

**Practice:** Before responding, pause and ask your body.
