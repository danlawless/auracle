# Mantras for the Moment

## Grounding Phrases for Living in Your Body

*These are words you can return to when presence feels like too much—or too little.*

---

### When You Doubt Your Energy Is Real

- What I feel in a room is information.
- My body knows before my mind catches up.
- I don't have to explain what I sense to trust it.
- My field is trainable, not mystical.
- I am allowed to work with what others can't see.

---

### When You Take On What Isn't Yours

- Not every emotion in the room is mine to carry.
- I can feel others deeply without absorbing them.
- I clear what isn't mine. I keep what is.
- My energy is my responsibility. Theirs is theirs.
- I can be connected and still be boundaried.

---

### When You're Afraid of Your Own Power

- Power without control is still safe.
- I can be magnetic and still be kind.
- My presence is allowed to affect the room.
- I don't have to manage how others receive me.
- Coherence is not manipulation. It's integrity.

---

### When Desire Feels Dangerous

- Wanting is not weakness.
- My desire is not a threat.
- I can feel longing without acting from urgency.
- Sexuality is life force—and it belongs to me.
- I don't have to suppress myself to be safe.

---

### When Intimacy Overwhelms

- I can be close without losing myself.
- Connection doesn't require collapse.
- I can receive without performing.
- Slowness is not rejection. It's care.
- My body sets the pace. I trust it.

---

### When Your "Yes" or "No" Isn't Clear

- My body will tell me if I listen.
- Confusion is a signal, not a destination.
- I can pause. I can wait. I can feel.
- A reluctant yes is still a no.
- I trust the signal, even when it's quiet.

---

### When You Perform Instead of Embody

- I don't have to work this hard to be seen.
- My presence is enough without proving.
- I can lead without managing perception.
- Stillness is more magnetic than effort.
- I am not performing power. I am it.

---

### When Others Misread You

- I don't control what they project onto me.
- Their interpretation of my energy is not my assignment.
- I can remain coherent when others misunderstand.
- Misperception is not evidence that I'm wrong.
- I stay in my body. That's enough.

---

### The Foundation Mantras

*Return to these when nothing else reaches you:*

- My aura is not mystical first—it's physiological first.
- My presence is erotic when it belongs to me.
- I don't manage my impact. I trust it.
- When I regulate internally, I radiate externally.
- I am not adding to who I am. I am letting who I am become visible.

---

*These mantras are anchors in the body. Let them land, not just be read.*
