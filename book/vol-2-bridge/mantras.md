# Mantras for the Moment

## Grounding Phrases for Healing

*These are words you can return to when the crossing feels impossible.*

---

### When Healing Feels Like Failure

- Slow is not stuck.
- I am not behind. I am between.
- Healing is not linear, and I am not lost.
- Every setback is a layer, not a starting over.
- I don't have to be fixed to be whole.

---

### When You Miss What Hurt You

- I can grieve something that wasn't good for me.
- Missing them doesn't mean I was wrong to leave.
- My nervous system was trained on intensity—calm takes practice.
- Longing is not proof of love. It's proof of a bond.
- I can honor the ache without returning to the source.

---

### When Safety Feels Boring

- Boredom might be peace I haven't learned to trust yet.
- Calm is not emptiness—it's regulation.
- I don't need chaos to feel alive.
- My nervous system is unlearning survival. Let it.
- I can feel deeply without being dysregulated.

---

### When Guilt Follows Boundaries

- I can stay kind without staying available.
- I can care without compensating.
- Distance is not abandonment.
- My peace is not negotiable for their comfort.
- I am allowed to choose myself and still be a good person.

---

### When the Body Remembers

- This feeling is a memory, not a present danger.
- My body is trying to protect me—even when the threat has passed.
- I can feel this without becoming it.
- Sensation is information, not instruction.
- What rises can also settle.

---

### When You Feel Unlovable

- My worth is not determined by who chose me—or who didn't.
- I am not too much. I was with too little.
- Being hard to love for the wrong person doesn't mean I'm hard to love.
- I don't have to earn belonging.
- I am learning that I was always worthy.

---

### When You Wonder If You'll Ever Be Okay

- I have survived every difficult day so far.
- My capacity for healing is as real as my capacity for pain.
- I don't have to see the other side to keep walking.
- This will not always feel this heavy.
- The bridge I'm crossing is real—even when I can't see the end.

---

### When Old Patterns Pull You Back

- I recognize this. That's progress.
- Noticing is the first step. I don't have to fix it all today.
- I can have compassion for the part of me that learned this.
- Awareness is not the same as agreement.
- I choose differently now—even when it's hard.

---

### The Foundation Mantras

*Return to these when nothing else reaches you:*

- Secure attachment is not found—it is practiced.
- My nervous system learned danger as connection. It can learn something else.
- I am not broken. I am between worlds.
- Healing requires new experiences, not more understanding.
- I am crossing the bridge. I don't have to see the other side to keep going.

---

*These mantras are companions, not cures. Take them with you on the crossing.*
