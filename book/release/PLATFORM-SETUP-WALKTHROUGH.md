## Platform 1: Amazon KDP

**Time:** 30-45 minutes
**URL:** https://kdp.amazon.com

### Step 1: Create/Login to Account
1. Go to kdp.amazon.com
2. Sign in with existing Amazon account OR create new one
3. Accept terms and conditions

### Step 2: Complete Tax Interview
**Have ready:** SSN or EIN

1. Click "Update" next to Tax Information
2. Select individual or business
3. Enter SSN/EIN
4. Complete W-9 equivalent form
5. Submit and wait for validation (usually instant)

### Step 3: Add Bank Account
1. Click "Update" next to Payment Information
2. Add bank account (routing + account number)
3. Verify details

### Step 4: Know Your Settings (for when you upload books)

**Royalty Option:** Choose 70% (for ebooks priced $2.99-$9.99)

**KDP Select Decision:**
- YES = 90 days Amazon exclusive, but access to Kindle Unlimited readers
- NO = Sell on Gumroad simultaneously
- *Recommendation:* Start with NO to build Gumroad email list
