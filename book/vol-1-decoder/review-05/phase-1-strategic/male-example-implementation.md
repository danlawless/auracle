## Draft Content for Review

### Sidebar: When Men Are Trapped

*Not all manipulation follows the same scripts. Consider this pattern:*

**The Baby Trap**

She got pregnant to keep him. Whether for resources, security, or simply to "own" him, the pregnancy wasn't about starting a family—it was about ending his options.

Some men stay out of love and duty, providing for a child while locked into a relationship they might otherwise have left. Others stay because they've constructed narratives to justify it: "It's a divine union." "This was meant to happen." The spiritual bypass gives meaning to what is actually coercion.

Watch for these patterns:
- Sudden pregnancy after you've expressed doubts about the relationship
- "Accidents" that happen when you're most vulnerable or committed
- Pressure to commit quickly once pregnancy is announced

**The Language Barrier as Control**

Some manipulators deliberately choose partners with limited English—not from genuine connection, but because the language barrier creates an advantage. If your partner struggles to articulate complex thoughts or catch subtle manipulation, you can be "always right."

If someone consistently pursues partners who can't effectively challenge them verbally, that's a pattern worth noticing.

*Recognition isn't gendered. Manipulation isn't either.*
