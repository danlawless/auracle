## Finding Support

### Types of Practitioners

- Family therapist with trauma training
- Parent-child interaction therapist
- Attachment-focused therapist
- Somatic therapist (for your own work)

### What to Look For

- Trauma-informed approach
- Understanding of attachment
- Non-judgmental stance toward parenting struggles
- Experience with intergenerational patterns

## Practice Support

### Daily

- Pause before reacting
- Name your own state
- Repair when you rupture

### Weekly

- Check in with your own needs
- Notice patterns emerging
- Seek support when needed
