# Course 1: Control Disguised as Care

## The Narcissism Decoder — A Course in Pattern Recognition

---

## Course Overview

**Stage:** SEE
**Duration:** ~12 hours
**Lessons:** 28 video lessons across 6 modules
**Core Insight:** *"This wasn't love—this was conditioning."*

This course teaches you to recognize manipulation patterns in real-time. You will learn to decode 56 tactics of control, understand why your nervous system got hooked, and reclaim your perception. By the end, you will trust yourself again.

---

## Learning Outcomes

By completing this course, you will:

1. **Recognize** — Identify manipulation tactics as they happen, not days later
2. **Understand** — Know why these patterns hook your nervous system
3. **Decode** — Use practical tools to see through control disguised as care
4. **Respond** — React from power rather than automatic reaction
5. **Recover** — Trust your own perception again
6. **Reclaim** — Take back your sovereignty and right to peace

---

## Who This Course Is For

- Anyone who has left interactions feeling worse about themselves without understanding why
- Those wondering if they're "too sensitive" or if something really is wrong
- People actively recovering from narcissistic abuse in any relationship
- Those beginning to see family dynamics, friendships, or work relationships with new eyes
- Anyone who wants to recognize patterns before getting pulled in
- Therapists, coaches, and healers who support survivors

## How to Take This Course

### Recommended Pace
- 2-3 lessons per week
- Complete each module before moving to the next
- Allow 1-2 days between heavy lessons for integration

### For Each Lesson
1. Find a quiet, private space
2. Have your journal ready
3. Watch without multitasking
4. Complete the exercises before moving on
5. Practice in real life before the next lesson

### Self-Care Protocol
This material may activate memories and emotions. This is normal and part of the healing process. If overwhelmed:
- Pause the lesson
- Use the 3-3-3 Rule
- Ground with the exercises in Lesson 5.1
- Return when ready

## Quick Reference

**The 3-3-3 Rule**
- 3 Seconds: Pause before responding
- 3 Breaths: Inhale 4, exhale 6
- 3 Words or fewer: "I'll think about that"

**The Master Question**
> "When was the last time you were genuinely wrong, admitted it without being forced to, and changed your behavior as a result?"

**The 72-Hour Test**
Wait 72 hours before major decisions about someone. Love-bombing neurochemistry needs time to settle.
