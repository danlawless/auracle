## Card 3: Nervous System States

### The Three States

| State | Signs | Field Quality | What Helps |
|-------|-------|---------------|------------|
| **Ventral (Safe)** | Relaxed, open, present, curious | Coherent, inviting, warm | Maintain |

### Integration Line

*"My field is not separate from my body. When I regulate inside, I radiate outside."*
