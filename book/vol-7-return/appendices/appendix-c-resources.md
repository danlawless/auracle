## Financial Recovery Support

### Professionals to Consider

- Financial therapist (combines therapy with financial planning)
- Fee-only financial advisor (no commission bias)
- Credit counselor (nonprofit)
- Attorney (for divorce/separation financial issues)

---

## Books for Recovery

- *The Soul of Money* by Lynne Twist
- *Your Money or Your Life* by Vicki Robin
- *Mind Over Money* by Klontz & Klontz
- *Crossing the Unknown Sea* by David Whyte

---

## Practice Integration

### Daily

- Notice scarcity thoughts without acting
- Practice receiving without obligation
- Track spending from curiosity, not judgment

### Weekly

- Review relationship with money
- Notice where urgency drives decisions
- Celebrate one sovereign financial choice
