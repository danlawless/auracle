# Appendix C: Resources

## For Continued Learning and Support

---

## Professional Development

### Supervision

All guides benefit from ongoing supervision. Look for:
- Experienced practitioners in your modality
- Trauma-informed perspective
- Understanding of countertransference

### Continued Education

- Trauma training
- Ethics workshops
- Somatic approaches
- Cultural competency

---

## Crisis Resources for Clients

Know these and have them accessible:

**National Suicide Prevention Lifeline**: 988

**Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741

**SAMHSA National Helpline**: 1-800-662-4357

## Books for Guides

- *On Being a Therapist* by Jeffrey Kottler
- *Therapeutic Presence* by Geller & Greenberg
- *The Drama of the Gifted Child* by Alice Miller
- *Ethics in Psychotherapy and Counseling* by Pope & Vasquez

---

## Practice Hygiene

### Before Sessions

- Clear your field
- Ground in your body
- Set intention for service

### After Sessions

- Release what isn't yours
- Process if needed (supervision)
- Return to yourself
