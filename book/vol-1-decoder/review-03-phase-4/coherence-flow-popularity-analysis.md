## Purpose

This analysis provides quantitative and qualitative ranking of all chapters and sections by:

1. **Coherence** — Internal consistency, logical structure, clear throughlines
2. **Flow** — Reading rhythm, transitions, pacing, accessibility
3. **Section Popularity** — Predicted reader engagement, utility, and impact

These rankings help identify which sections may need additional attention and which serve as models for the rest of the manuscript.
