## Summary

These recommendations aim to strengthen an already powerful manuscript by:

1. **Fixing logical flow** (moving foundational content earlier)
2. **Reducing cognitive overload** (splitting dense chapters)
3. **Protecting emotional impact** (ending on climax, not protocols)
4. **Improving usability** (consolidating, adding references)
5. **Filling gaps** (siblings, co-parenting, economic abuse)
6. **Enhancing reader experience** (transitions, processing breaks)

The content is exceptional. These structural adjustments will help that content shine.
