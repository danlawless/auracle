# Vol. 1 Reorganization Plan: Combined Decoder Playbook

## Overview

This plan combines the Narcissist Playbook (Chapter 3) with the Decoder Cards (Chapters 14-15) into a single unified chapter, reorganized to:

1. **Lead with the most common tactics** (Cards 1-20)
2. **Break up heavy content after Card 20** with field notes, grounding exercises, and quotes
3. **Separate substance abuse patterns** into their own dedicated section
4. **Reduce mental fatigue** through strategic pacing
