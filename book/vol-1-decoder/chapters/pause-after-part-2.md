Before continuing, take a moment:

## Notice Your Body

You've just traveled through territory that may have been familiar—painfully familiar. Family wounds, childhood patterns, manipulation across every context of life.

Where is this living in your body right now? Shoulders? Chest? Stomach? Jaw?

Place a hand there. Breathe into it. You don't have to fix anything. Just witness.

## Notice Your Emotions

What's arising?

Perhaps grief for the child you were. Perhaps anger at what was done. Perhaps relief at finally having words for experiences you couldn't name. Perhaps exhaustion from carrying this alone for so long.

All of it is valid. All of it belongs.

## Ground Yourself

Three slow breaths. Feet on floor. Hand on heart.

You are not in that family system anymore. You are here, now, an adult who gets to choose. The past is information, not destiny.

---
