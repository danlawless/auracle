## ðŸ“– How to Read This Book

This book is not about becoming more powerful as a helper.

It is about becoming sustainable.

If you have completed the earlier volumes, you've done the work. Now you feel called to help others. This book teaches you to do that without burning out, without losing yourself, and without recreating unhealthy dynamics.

---

## ðŸŒŸ The Promise

By the end of this book, you will:

1. **Understand** why trauma survivors are drawn to helping roles
2. **Recognize** when helping becomes a way to avoid your own healing
3. **Navigate** the ethics of lived-experience guidance
4. **Build** practices that protect both helper and helped
5. **Maintain** energy hygiene and prevent burnout
6. **Know** when to refer and how to do it gracefully
7. **Create** content that serves without performing
8. **Sustain** a helping practice for the long term

---

## ðŸ’¬ A Note Before You Begin

> *You feel called to help.*
>
> *Maybe it's because you know what it's like to be in the dark. Maybe it's because someone helped you, and you want to pass that forward. Maybe people just show up at your door with their pain, and you've never known how to turn them away.*
