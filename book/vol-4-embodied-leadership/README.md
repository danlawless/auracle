## How to Read This Book

This book is not about becoming more powerful.

It is about allowing the power you already have to become coherent.

If you have completed Sovereignty (Volume 3), something new is possible:

- Your boundaries are installed
- Your nervous system is regulated
- Your choices are your own
- You're ready to live from presence, not just protect it

This book teaches you how to embody that.

---

## The Promise

By the end of this book, you will:

1. **Understand** your energy field as trainable, not mystical
2. **Practice** daily aura hygiene that clears what isn't yours
3. **Embody** boundaries that live inside the body
4. **Reclaim** sexual energy as life force, not survival strategy
5. **Navigate** intimacy without self-loss or collapse
6. **Lead** through coherence rather than tactics
7. **Recognize** the shadow side of healing and helping
8. **Live** sovereignty as identity, not practice
