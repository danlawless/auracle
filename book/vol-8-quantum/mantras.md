# Mantras for the Moment

## Grounding Phrases for Becoming

*These are words you can return to when the unveiling feels like too much—or not enough.*

---

### When You Feel Called to Be Smaller

- I am done pretending to be limited.
- I don't shrink to make others comfortable anymore.
- My vastness is not a threat.
- I can take up the space I'm meant to take.
- I was made for this.

---

### When Fear Arrives

- Fear is excitement without breath.
- I can feel afraid and still move forward.
- This trembling is at the edge of possibility—not danger.
- Fear means I'm close to something that matters.
- I breathe into it and let it transform.

---

### When You Try to Control

- I can allow instead of manage.
- Control was how I survived. Trust is how I live.
- I release what I cannot hold.
- Life knows where it's going. I can follow.
- I align instead of force.

---

### When Desire Arises

- Desire is not lack. It's direction.
- I follow what calls me.
- Wanting is allowed.
- I don't have to justify what I'm drawn toward.
- I trust the pull.

---

### When Synchronicity Speaks

- This isn't coincidence. It's conversation.
- I'm paying attention.
- Life is responding to my alignment.
- The signs are real. I let myself see them.
- I follow the thread.

---

### When the Old Self Resists

- What dissolves was never the truth of me.
- I let go of who I had to be.
- Shedding is not loss. It's revelation.
- The small self was a costume. I'm removing it.
- I grieve the disguise without returning to it.

---

### When You Lose the Feeling

- I don't have to feel quantum to be quantum.
- Glimpses are enough. They compound.
- I return to the practice, not the feeling.
- The sky doesn't stop existing when clouds cover it.
- I trust the truth I've touched, even when I can't reach it now.

---

### When It All Feels Unreal

- This is not fantasy. This is physics.
- My skepticism doesn't diminish what I've experienced.
- I can doubt and still stay open.
- Ordinary magic is still ordinary. That's what makes it real.
- I don't need to believe all of it to live from what I know.

---

### When You're on the Threshold

- I don't have to rush the crossing.
- The threshold is part of the journey, not an obstacle to it.
- I can be between and still be whole.
- Something is emerging. I let it.
- I am ready—even when I don't feel ready.

---

### The Foundation Mantras

*Return to these when nothing else reaches you:*

- I was never becoming. I was always unveiling.
- I am the universe looking at itself.
- Fear into fuel. Desire into direction.
- I am the sky, not the storms that pass through.
- What I've been pretending not to be—I am.

---

*These mantras are whispers from the version of you that already knows. Listen.*
