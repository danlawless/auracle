## Analysis Framework

Each chapter is evaluated on:
- **Function:** What role does this chapter play?
- **Pacing:** How does the chapter flow?
- **Earns Its Place:** Does unique content justify inclusion?
- **Hook Quality:** Does the opening grab readers?
- **Strengths:** What works well?
- **Concerns:** What needs attention?
- **Recommendations:** Specific revision suggestions
