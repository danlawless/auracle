# Mantras for the Moment

## Grounding Phrases for Building a Life

*These are words you can return to when prosperity feels impossible or forbidden.*

---

### When Scarcity Feels True

- My nervous system believes there's never enough. That belief can change.
- Scarcity is a pattern, not a prophecy.
- What I feel right now is history, not destiny.
- I survived scarcity. I don't have to stay loyal to it.
- Abundance doesn't require forgetting what I went through.

---

### When Receiving Feels Dangerous

- I am allowed to receive.
- I don't have to earn everything through struggle.
- Ease is not a trick.
- I can accept good things without waiting for the catch.
- My hands can stay open.

---

### When Success Triggers Fear

- I can be visible and still be safe.
- Thriving is not a betrayal of those who didn't.
- I don't have to shrink to stay loved.
- Success can coexist with integrity.
- I'm allowed to have more than I had before.

---

### When You Feel Like an Imposter

- My gaps are not proof I don't belong.
- My unconventional path brought me here. That's valid.
- I don't owe anyone a seamless resume.
- I can own what I know and name what I don't.
- I am qualified by experience, not just credentials.

---

### When Work Wants to Consume You

- Work was how I survived. It doesn't have to be how I live.
- I am more than what I produce.
- Rest is not laziness. It's reclamation.
- My value exists apart from my output.
- I can do less and still be enough.

---

### When Money Feels Shameful

- Wealth is not inherently corrupt.
- Wanting money is not betraying my values.
- I can be prosperous and still be good.
- Money is a tool. I decide how to use it.
- Financial safety is not greed.

---

### When Building Feels Impossible

- I don't have to see the whole picture to take the next step.
- I am allowed to build something new.
- I can start from where I am, not where I should be.
- Small steps are still steps.
- My foundation is forming even when I can't see it.

---

### When You Feel Behind

- I am not behind. I was surviving.
- My timeline is not a measure of my worth.
- What I lost can be rebuilt differently.
- I release the comparison to where I "should" be.
- My path brought me here. That's all it needed to do.

---

### The Foundation Mantras

*Return to these when nothing else reaches you:*

- I am allowed to thrive, not just survive.
- Prosperity is not a betrayal of my healing. It's a fruit of it.
- My outer life is allowed to match my inner work.
- What I build now is mine.
- I have survived enough. I am allowed to live.

---

*These mantras are permissions. Take them. They were always yours.*
